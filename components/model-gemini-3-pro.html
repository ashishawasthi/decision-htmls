Gemini 3 Pro in agentic systems

The highest-quality Gemini in this lineup, for the hardest reasoning, long-context synthesis, and judging. It costs more and starts slower than the Flash tiers. The design rule that follows: reach for it only on the steps where quality clearly outranks cost and latency, not as a default for every call.

When to pick it

Good fit

  • The hardest reasoning, planning, and long-context synthesis steps.
  • A judge or verifier in a quality gate, where a stronger model checks a cheaper one.
  • Complex multimodal tasks that the Flash tiers cannot carry alone.

Avoid

  • Sub-second SLOs and high-frequency, simple traffic: use Flash-Lite or Flash.
  • As the fast lane in smart routing: its price and time to first token defeat the point.

How it maps to this tool

  • Reasoning model for quality-first designs. Because the Generation stage is model-aware, choosing it raises the modelled time to first token and the blended generation latency, and its higher output price shows in the cost panel. Keep Flash-Lite as the fast lane.
  • Integrated search tool: yes. Google Search grounding, so live web grounding runs when a web source is added, billed about $14 per 1k searches.
  • Cross-model judge. A strong pick for Cross-model judge diversity, where a stronger model checks a cheaper one in the quality gate.
