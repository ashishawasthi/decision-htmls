## Estimates only, and they go stale

The tools in this repository produce rough, deterministic estimates for
planning and educational purposes. They are not exact, and they are not a
substitute for official sources.

- Cost figures, model catalogs, and pricing reflect publicly available
  information at roughly May 2026 and will drift out of date as vendors change
  prices, regions, quotas, and product lineups.
- GPU memory and out-of-memory predictions are calibrated heuristics. Real
  runs vary with framework versions, drivers, kernel choices, batch shapes,
  and hardware. Treat any "fits" result as a starting hypothesis, not a
  guarantee.
- Architecture sizing and "what breaks at scale" notes are simplified models,
  not a replacement for load testing your own workload.

Always verify against the official, current source before you make a
purchasing, capacity, or design decision. For cost and product details, check
Google Cloud pricing and the Vertex AI documentation directly. For memory and
performance, run your own benchmarks.
